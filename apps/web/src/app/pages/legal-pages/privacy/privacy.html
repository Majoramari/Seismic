Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Seismic collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use our website, dashboard, leaderboard, editor extension, and API (the "Service"). We are committed to complying with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Who We Are

Seismic operates the coding-activity tracking service available at seismic.icu. For the purposes of the GDPR, Seismic acts as the data controller for the personal data described in this policy.

Contact: hello@seismic.icu

Where Your Data Is Stored

All personal data collected through the Service is stored on servers located within the European Union. We do not transfer your personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA), except where a third-party sub-processor requires it and only under safeguards recognized by the GDPR (such as Standard Contractual Clauses), which we will disclose if applicable.

What Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

Account data

  • Email address (for login and verification)
  • Username and display name
  • No password is collected — Seismic works without passwords; you sign in through a passwordless verification link/code sent to your email

Coding-activity data (collected via the editor extension)

  • Time spent coding
  • Project names and file types
  • Programming languages used
  • Timestamps of activity

Profile and preference data

  • Privacy settings (e.g., public profile, hide time, hide projects, hide languages, hide from leaderboard)
  • Personal goals you set (e.g., weekly coding-time targets)
  • API key (used to connect your editor to your account)

Technical data

  • Login timestamps and session information

We do not collect the actual content of your code — only metadata about time, project names, and languages.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Operate your dashboard, goals, and personal statistics
  • Calculate and display leaderboard rankings, only for users who opt in
  • Maintain your public profile, if you choose to make it public
  • Authenticate your editor extension via your API key
  • Send account-related emails (such as verification or security notices)
  • Maintain and improve the security and reliability of the Service

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising or share it with advertisers.

Who We Share Data With

We may share limited data with:

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers in the EU, who process data on our behalf as processors under a data processing agreement.
  • Other users, but only the specific data you choose to make public (via public profile or leaderboard settings).
  • Authorities, if legally required to comply with a valid legal request.

We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Data Retention

  • We retain your account and coding-activity data for as long as your account is active.
  • If you reset your stats, all tracked coding-time data is permanently deleted immediately and cannot be recovered.
  • If you delete your account, we permanently delete your personal data and coding-activity data from our active systems immediately, at the moment of deletion.
  • Backups containing your data are rotated and fully purged within a reasonable period after deletion.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") — delete your account and personal data at any time, directly from your account settings, with no need to contact support.
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — for anything based on consent (e.g., public leaderboard visibility), at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint — with the data protection authority in your EU country of residence.

To exercise these rights, use the in-app "Delete account" or "Reset stats" options in Settings, or contact us at hello@seismic.icu. We will respond within one month, as required by the GDPR.

Self-Service Data Control

Seismic is built so you can manage your own data directly, without needing to wait on us:

  • Reset stats — permanently deletes all your tracked coding time, instantly, from Settings → Account.
  • Delete account — permanently deletes your account and all associated personal data, instantly, from Settings → Account (requires typing "delete" to confirm, to prevent accidental deletion).
  • Privacy toggles — control what parts of your data (if any) are visible to others, at any time, from Settings → Privacy & Security.

Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure passwordless authentication, and access controls limiting who can view your data internally. No system is 100% secure, but we continuously work to protect your information.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us so we can delete it.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies necessary for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will notify you by email or an in-app notice before they take effect.

Contact Us

For any privacy questions, requests, or complaints:

Email: hello@seismic.icu

You may also contact your local data protection authority if you believe your rights under GDPR have not been respected.
