Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Seismic collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use our website, dashboard, leaderboard, editor integrations, and API.

Who We Are

Seismic operates a coding-activity tracking service. For privacy questions, contact us at hello@seismic.icu.

What Data We Collect

We collect the information needed to run your account and activity dashboard:

  • Account data such as email address, username, and display name.
  • Coding activity metadata such as time, project names, file paths, languages, editor, branch, OS, machine, line count, cursor line, timezone, and keystroke count.
  • Profile settings, privacy settings, goals, badges, and API keys.
  • Import data you choose to upload, such as WakaTime history.

Seismic does not collect the contents of your source code.

How We Use Your Data

  • To operate your dashboard, project summaries, timelines, goals, badges, and leaderboard.
  • To authenticate editor integrations through your API key.
  • To provide public profile data only when your privacy settings allow it.
  • To send account-related emails such as sign-in and security messages.
  • To maintain service reliability and prevent abuse.

We do not sell your personal data or use it for advertising.

Public Data and Privacy Controls

You control what appears publicly. Settings can hide your time, projects, languages, editors, operating systems, badges, leaderboard presence, and selected projects.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • We retain account and activity data while your account is active.
  • Resetting stats deletes tracked coding-time data.
  • Deleting your account deletes account data and associated activity data.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, restrict, object to, or delete your personal data. You can manage most data directly in Settings or contact us at hello@seismic.icu.

Security

We use HTTPS/TLS, passwordless authentication, scoped API keys, and access controls to help protect your data. No online service can guarantee perfect security, but we work to keep Seismic reliable and safe.

Cookies

Seismic uses essential cookies and browser storage for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as Seismic changes. If changes are material, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users.
